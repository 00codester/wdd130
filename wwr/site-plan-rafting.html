Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to build more revenue for the company by having information about the white water rapids and the company more readily available to consumers. The intent is to draw in more consumers through exciting pictures, testimonies, and information about the rapids.

Audience

The audience for my website are single men or women in their 20's to 30's who are looking for an adrenaline rush to spice up their lives. Either with friends or solo is fine. In this age group the site will be accessed on laptops or mobile devices. Mainly laptops for booking, but possibly mobile devices for initial access or information seeking.
