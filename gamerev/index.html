Our Mission

Our mission is to review lesser-known video games with the intent to inform the viewer or reader about a potential video game to play. We will cover the basic premise behind the games story (if a story exists). We want readers or viewers to understand the full state of the game. Our reviews will touch on known bugs or issues with the current state of the game. We will touch on how well it fulfils the intended niche. Allowing our readers to gain a better understanding of the game will help their overall satisfaction with their purchase.
